The Challenge

Respiratory therapy clinical education ran entirely on paper.

Across hundreds of US respiratory therapy programs, student clinical education — patient simulations, competency assessments, and rotation tracking — was managed on printed forms, spreadsheets, and email. There was no dedicated digital platform in the entire category.

Clinical Trac's founders had a clear vision and an existing network of school relationships — but needed a product that could onboard programs quickly and handle HIPAA-sensitive student data from day one.

Why OnPoint Nexus

Healthcare compliance expertise and a 45-day MVP commitment.

Clinical Trac needed a team that understood HIPAA requirements at the architecture level — not as a checkbox. OnPoint's HealthTech track record made them the natural choice. The 45-day MVP target gave Clinical Trac enough time to pilot with their first five schools before expanding.

"Within six months of launch, 52 schools had onboarded their respiratory therapy programs. The platform OnPoint built is now central to how we deliver clinical education across the US."

— Sarah K., Co-Founder · Clinical Trac Inc.

What We Built

The first digital platform for clinical respiratory education.

Curriculum Delivery

Digital course delivery, patient simulation scenarios, and competency assessment tools — replacing paper-based clinical education for the first time in the category.

Competency Tracking

Student progress dashboards, clinical rotation tracking, and HIPAA-compliant patient encounter logging — with supervisor sign-off workflows built in.

School Onboarding

Self-serve school registration, program configuration, and instructor management — designed to take a new respiratory therapy program from signup to active use in under 30 minutes.

52 schools. 6 months. A new category created.
