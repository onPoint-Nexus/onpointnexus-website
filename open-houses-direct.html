The Challenge

The open house was the most important real estate event with no dedicated digital home.

Home buyers in active markets wanted to find and track open houses across an entire neighbourhood in a single weekend. Agents wanted to broadcast listings to genuinely interested buyers — not just MLS scraper apps. Neither had a purpose-built tool.

Zillow and Realtor.com had open house features, but they were buried. The founder saw a clear gap: a standalone app designed entirely around the open house moment, for both sides of the transaction.

Why OnPoint Nexus

Native iOS + Android + web. One team. 55 days.

Open Houses Direct needed three platforms launched simultaneously — native iOS, native Android, and a web listing portal for agents. Most agencies would phase these across 6–12 months. OnPoint proposed building all three in parallel with a dedicated cross-platform team and delivered inside the budget and timeline.

"Every other agency told me to pick one platform first. OnPoint was the only team that said they could do all three simultaneously — and proved it."

— Founder, Open Houses Direct

What We Built

A new category in real estate technology.

Map-First Discovery

Real-time open house listings on an interactive map — filter by date, price, property type, and neighbourhood. Saved searches with push notifications for new listings.

Agent Broadcast Tools

Agent dashboard for posting and promoting open houses — targeting buyers by saved search criteria in the local area. Real-time RSVP tracking and attendee messaging.

Native iOS & Android

Full native apps — Swift for iOS, Kotlin for Android — not a web wrapper. Optimised for the on-the-go nature of open house weekends with offline capability and background location.

The Results

First-to-market. Live on App Store and Google Play.
